Your Guide

About Rodrigo

A Lisbon native, storyteller, and passionate guide who believes the best way to know a city is to walk it.

The Guide

A Local's Love for Lisbon

"I grew up in these streets. Every azulejo, every steep alley, every viewpoint — they hold a memory. I just want to share mine with you."

Rodrigo was born in Toronto Ontario (CA), and raised in three continents, by the time he was 17 years old. By this time, he settled in Lisbon, where he’s spent his adult life doing what he loves most: exploring the city and uncovering its stories. As a guide for TakingUThere, he developed a reputation for combining deep historical knowledge with genuine warmth and a storyteller's instinct for what makes history come alive.

He studied Political Science in Lisbon, and has spent years building a repertoire that goes far beyond dates and monuments. He knows the names of the women who hung laundry in Alfama's alleys three generations ago. He can point to the exact café where Fernando Pessoa wrote his most melancholic poems. He knows which pastelaria has been making the same recipe since 1837.

His tours are conversations, not lectures. He asks questions, encourages curiosity, and takes detours - because the best discoveries are always the unplanned ones.

Philosophy

How Rodrigo Guides

Storytelling First

History only sticks when it's told as a story. Rodrigo focuses on the human element — the people, the conflicts, the love stories — behind every monument and square.

Small Groups

Maximum 8–10 guests per tour. This isn't a crowd experience — it's an intimate conversation with the city, designed so everyone can ask questions and explore at their own pace.

Off the Beaten Path

Every route includes at least one or two surprises that aren't in any guidebook — because Lisbon's soul lives in the details that most visitors walk straight past.

Genuine Connection

Rodrigo genuinely loves what he does — and it shows. His guests consistently describe his tours as the most memorable part of their visit to Lisbon.
