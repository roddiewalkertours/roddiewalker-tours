Your Guide

Meet Rodrigo

"Lisbon is a city that rewards the curious — every tile, every alley has a story waiting to be told."

Born and raised in Lisbon, Rodrigo has spent years studying the city's history, architecture, and culture — and even more time exploring its streets. With experience guiding for TakingUThere, he brings a rare combination of academic depth and genuine local warmth to every tour.

He believes that the best way to understand a city is through its people, its food, and the unexpected details that guidebooks miss.
