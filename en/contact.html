FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear and bring? +

Comfortable shoes are essential — Lisbon has cobblestone streets and hills. Bring water, sunscreen in summer and a light layer for the evening. The rest is taken care of.

What is the cancellation policy? +

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the tour. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a 50% fee. In case of bad weather affecting the experience, tours can be rescheduled at no cost.

Are the tours suitable for children? +

Yes! Rodrigo adapts his storytelling to the group. Children up to 12 are welcome on family tours with a reduced rate. Contact us to discuss the best option.

Where do the tours start? +

Each tour has its own meeting point, confirmed in the booking confirmation. Most start at accessible, central locations near public transport.
