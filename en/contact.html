FAQ

Common Questions

What should I wear and bring? +

Comfortable walking shoes are essential — Lisbon has cobblestones and hills. Bring water, sunscreen in summer, and a light layer for evenings. Everything else is taken care of.

What is your cancellation policy? +

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the tour. Cancellations within 48 hours may incur a 50% fee. In case of bad weather affecting the experience, tours can be rescheduled at no charge.

Are tours suitable for children? +

Yes! Rodrigo adapts his storytelling to suit the group. Children under 12 are welcome on family tours at a reduced rate. Contact us to discuss the best option.

Where do tours start? +

Each tour has its own meeting point, confirmed in your booking confirmation. Most start in accessible, central locations near public transport.
